Delete Your CurioCity Account

We're sorry to see you go. Follow the instructions below to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

üì± Method 1: In-App Deletion RECOMMENDED

The fastest and easiest way to delete your account is directly through the CurioCity app:

  1. Open the CurioCity app on your device
  2. Sign in to your account if you haven't already
  3. Navigate to your Profile (tap the profile icon in the bottom navigation)
  4. Scroll down to the Account section
  5. Tap the "Delete Account" button
  6. Read the warning and tap "Continue"
  7. Confirm by tapping "Delete My Account"
  8. If you signed up with email/password, enter your password when prompted
  9. Your account will be permanently deleted immediately

If you're unable to access the app or prefer to contact us directly:

  3. We'll verify your identity and process the deletion within 48 hours
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email once your account is deleted
