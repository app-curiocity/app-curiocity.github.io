Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are important to us.

inventory Data We Collect

ads_click Advertising and Analytics

CurioCity displays ads through Google AdMob to support free app usage. Our advertising partners collect data to provide personalized ads:

Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
IP address and approximate location
App usage patterns and ad interactions
Crash reports and performance data
info Opt-out Available: You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings or through ad preferences.

security Data Security

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS encryption. Your account data is securely stored using Firebase's enterprise-grade security infrastructure.

Analyze app usage for advertising optimization
Provide customer support when needed

share Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may only be shared with:

Service providers (Firebase, Google services) under strict privacy agreements
Advertising partners (Google AdMob, ad networks) for personalized advertising
Analytics providers for app performance and crash reporting
Legal authorities when required by law
Other users only for data you explicitly choose to share (tour groups, public tours)

For advertising, we share device identifiers and usage data with Google AdMob and their advertising partners. This data is used to display relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices at policies.google.com/privacy.
